## Product

Infra for web3 games. Marketplace for game items and currencies. Runs on their
own appchain, built on Base.

"Open Loot is a premier Web3 gaming platform that empowers studios to build
player-driven economies. We provide custody services, global fiat and crypto
payments, KYC/AML, risk management, a leading marketplace, integrated customer
support, a PC game launcher, and user acquisition tools."

Games on their platform as of December 2025:

- [Big Time](/screenings/big-time/)
- WorldShards
- Trump Billionaires Club

Started 2022. Token launched 2024. Appchain in 2025.
