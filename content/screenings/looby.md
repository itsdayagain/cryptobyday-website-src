Looby is an art-focused memecoin created by ex-Rockstar Games senior artist Stephen
Bliss. The art amazing, the best I've seen in any memecoin. If they can define
and communicate their identity better, my verdict will go from okay to good.

<!--more-->

## Verdict: OKAY

- Amazing art.
- Very low market cap.
- No cabal bullshit.
- Lacks both personality and narrative.

With their skill on the art side, they could define and dominate the "artcoin"
category. But only if they can define and communicate their identity better.

## Product

Art-focused memecoin on Solana. All art is created and hand drawn by Stephen
Bliss.

"Our mission is to create a community-driven brand that fuses art, blockchain,
and storytelling into a lasting universe beyond memecoins."

- NFT collection:
  - 8% royalty
  - Half to project, half to holders
  - Hold 100,000 $LOOBY to qualify
- Comics
- Merch

Art looks amazing. Original. Stylish. Merch looks good. 

There is no personality here though. Who is Looby? What does he stand for? The
comics look good, but this lack of personality makes them emotionally not
interesting.
