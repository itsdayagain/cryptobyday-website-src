## Product

Decentralized, censorship resistant, permissionless infra for cross-chain
communication.

Special LayerZero smart contracts run on all integrated chains, controlled by
the LayerZero network of oracles, validators, and executors. Two-way
communication: a smart contract on the source chain can both read and write data
from/to the target chain.

This can be used to write omnichain apps that can read and write data, and
execute smart contracts across multiple blockchains, as if they were a single
chain.

This can be used for example to bridge tokens, and to receive price information
from other chains.

The core smart contracts deployed across chains are immutable and
non-upgradeable, to ensure the protocol will always work.

Integrated across the board with all the chains that matter (and most that
don't, too).

Aquired Stargate, a cross-chain bridge built on LayerZero, in August 2025.

Used by Ethena, EtherFi, Pancakeswap, Beam, Kraken, etc.

## Token

Token $ZRO. Launched mid 2024. Used to pay transaction fees, and for staking.
Fees do not have to be paid in ZRO, however.

55% of supply for investors/partners and team. Started vesting in mid 2025, ends
mid 2027.

Do monthly buyback and burns since September 2025 using revenue from Stargate.
100% of Stargate revenue will be used to buy ZRO on the open market and burn
them. As of December 2025, there have been 3 buybacks, with 1.2M USD total
spent.

There is also a fee switch mechanism, which enables additional fees in the
protocol. When enabled, these fees are converted to ZRO and burned. This switch
is controlled by ZRO holders to turn on or off in governance votes. So far it
has not been turned on, as the quorum has not been reached in any vote.
