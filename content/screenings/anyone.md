## Product

Decentralized VPN. Onion routed.

Can run on existing hardware. They also sell a custom wifi access point device
for $500, the "Anyone Router". It can be used for VPN access alone, but can also
be set up to share bandwidth to earn tokens.

Mainnet live 22 Dec 2025. 7.6k relays (router nodes) as of 2026-01-04.

I read the whitepaper. It was decent on the tech parts. What I'm missing is the
analysis of the business and demand side of things. It's one thing to build the
basic network, but another to get people to use it.

## Token

Token $ANYONE. Standard DePIN setup: pay to use services, get paid to offer
them. The idea is that it will be free to regular users, with premium features
like higher performance requiring payment. Relay operators must stake some
tokens. In the future, 15% of revenue will be used to buy back and burn tokens.
