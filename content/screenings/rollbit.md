## Token

Token $RLB was airdropped for free to Rollbit users. Originally launched on
Solana in November 2021, they moved to Ethereum in 2023.

The token is only traded on Uniswap, no centralized exchanges.

Users can hold the token to get reduced trading fees and other perks.

Part of revenue is used to buy back and burn tokens. Burns are done every hour,
and can be seen here:

https://etherscan.io/token/0x046eee2cc3188071c02bfc1745a6b17c656e3f3d?a=0x0000000000000000000000000000000000000000

There is also a dashboard with burn stats: https://rollbit.com/rlb/buy-and-burn

As of December 2025, 3.2B tokens have been burnt, and only 1.8B remain. Over the
past 30 days, around $75k worth has been bought back and burnt every day.
