## Product

"Aurory Project is a collective of highly passionate game developers, delivering
immersive free-to-play Web3 games and experiences."

Gaming studio. Building a collection of games in the "Aurory" world, with
interoperable assets. The premier one being "Amiko Legends", a multiplayer
"creature-collection RPG". Collect and train "Amikos". Live beta open, game will
be released in 2026.

Multiple in-game assets: Aurorian (characters), Amikos (creatures), cosmetics,
consumables. "Standard" assets are off-chain, and can't be traded. "Prime"
assets are on-chain, and can be traded.

All on-chain assets can be traded on the Aurory marketplace.

Running on Solana, but also has a "SyncSpace" item database which runs
off-chain. This can be used for cross-chain interop.

Players do not need a crypto wallet to get started.

Uses a "live development" process, where the community is involved very early.

The documentation site is very good. Clear and concise.

## Token

Token "$AURY" is the main ecosystem token. Will be used in every game. 

It is used as the marketplace currency, to give discounts, and to unlock some
content.

Two "non-tradable, off-chain sub-currencies": OKA and TOKE. They are used to
determine how to distribute $AURY rewards to players.

Finally, there is also "crystals", an in-game off-chain currency, that can be
used to buy items and access various functionality.

## Team

Large team, over 50 people listed on their website. HQ in San Francisco.

Many with backgrounds in the gaming industry. Executive producer is "Jonathan
Campeau", ex product director at Ubisoft Montreal.

Raised $100M in October 2021 from Animoca, Solana Ventures, among others.
