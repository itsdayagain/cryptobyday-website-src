## Product

"The Global Positioning Network for the Autonomous Future"

Decentralized network of sensors that provide RTK correction data for increased
location accuracy. Users run sensors (a.k.a. "miner" or "base station" hardware
nodes) and get rewarded with $GEOD tokens. Customers buy data, burning tokens.

RTK: Real-Time Kinematics. Gives centimeter-precision for GPS.

Started late 2021. As of December 2025 they have 20,585 nodes in 153 countries.

HQ in Silicon Valley.

The info on website and docs is very clear. YouTube explainer videos are very good.

## Token

Token $GEOD used for:

- Fees for data and other services.
- Rewards to miners.

80% of data revenue is used to buy back and burn tokens. Token burn has
increased exponentially since the start, with 29.6M tokens burnt as of Dec 2025
(1B total supply).

According to Blockworks, miners earn around 50 USD per month. Miners cost around
600-700 USD.
