## Product

Decentralized physical network for hi-def 3D mapping, to use with for example
spatial AI.

Data collectors buy hardware to provide data to the network, and customers buy
that data. They have two hardware devices: the TarantulaX and the LightCone.
TarantulaX is an RTK receiver for GEODNET, giving high accuracy geolocated video
when paired with a mobile phone. The LightCone is a full LiDAR device that you
put on your car, that generates 3D mapping data with 2 cm accuracy.

The network is in operation, with 2,155 active devices as of December 2025,
mostly covering USA, central Europe, and Thailand.

## Token

Token $ROVR. It is used to reward data collectors, and 60% of revenue goes to
buy back and burn tokens.

Burns started in November 2025, and so far 2.2M tokens have been burnt (out of a
total 10B). Burns are smaller than reward payouts, so far.

They also just launched a staking program, where you can stake $ROVR tokens to
redeem a hardware device. As of writing in December 2025, you can stake 300k
$ROVR to get a LightCone (about $2,500). Shipping in January 2026.
