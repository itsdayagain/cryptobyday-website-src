## Product

Decentralized wireless network, both wifi and 4G/5G. Contributors share their
Internet connection, using one of Roam's wifi miner hardware devices. Customers
download an app to their phone.

6.2 million wifi hotspots as of Dec 2025.

Started in late 2021 as MetaBlox, moved to Solana and rebranded to Roam in 2024.

## Token

Token $ROAM is used to reward miners, and to pay for access. There is a second
layer of "points" that are used as an intermediate currency. Big picture it's a
buyback and burn setup.
