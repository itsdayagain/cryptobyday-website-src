It's time for a change.

So I've been doing the TA Tuesday streams for a while now. It's been good, and
I've learnt a lot. But lately I've felt that they're kinda stagnating, and I
don't really enjoy doing them like I did starting out. Sure, TA is interesting,
but there's a whole lotta more things in crypto (and in general) that are just
as interesting and that I want to talk about, too.

Which means I'm at a bit of a crossroads here. Either I slog on with the Tuesday
charting, or I come up with something new, something more interesting. And by
that I mean more interesting to me, primarily. Because at the end of the day I'm
doing these streams for me. I mean, if I can deliver some value to you, that's
dope, and I love to teach and to connect. But from the start, this has been
something that I'm doing for myself first, others second.

The choice here seems pretty obvious: I need to lean in. So instead of just
talking about TA once a week, I'm gonna do a 30 minute stream every day, where I
cover a whole lot more. It's gonna be TA, yes, but it's also gonna be everything
else I find interesting in crypto right now, different things I'm working on,
HEGE, and just random thoughts that need to be shared.

So every evening at 16 UTC, Monday to Friday, I'm gonna connect my mic, share my
screen, and just talk about whatever happens to be on my mind. It's gonna be
unplanned and improvised. Sometimes it'll be good. Sometimes it'll be shit. But
alltimes it's gonna be 100% Day, that's my commitment to you, and to myself.

So if that vibes with you my friend, I invite you to tune in for 30 minutes of
my raw unfiltered thoughts on everything crypto (and more), every Monday through
Friday at 16:00 UTC, live on [my YouTube
channel](https://www.youtube.com/@cryptobyday) and in [HEGE's Telegram
channel](https://t.me/Hegequarters).
